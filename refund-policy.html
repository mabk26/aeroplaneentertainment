Refund Policy

Effective date: June 12, 2026 · Aeroplane Entertainment

14-day money-back guarantee. If CLICKO doesn't work for your use case, email us within 14 days of your first payment for a full refund — no questions asked.

CLICKO is provided by Aeroplane Entertainment as a monthly subscription (Basic, Pro, or Ultra tiers). We want you to be confident in your purchase.

Refund window

You may request a full refund within 14 days of your original purchase date by contacting us at support@aeroplaneentertainment.com. Please include the email address used at checkout.

Once approved, Lemon Squeezy processes the refund within 5–10 business days. Your Pro license is automatically revoked when Lemon Squeezy notifies our server of the refund.

Subscription cancellation

You can cancel your subscription at any time from the Lemon Squeezy customer portal (link in your purchase confirmation email). Cancellation stops future renewals. You retain Pro access until the end of your current billing period.

Renewals

Subscriptions renew automatically each month. You will receive an email receipt from Lemon Squeezy before each renewal. Cancel before the renewal date to avoid being charged.

Chargebacks and payment reversals

Chargebacks, payment reversals, or confirmed refunds automatically revoke Pro access for the associated email. If you believe a chargeback was made in error, contact us to resolve it.

AI credits

AI credits granted as part of a subscription are non-refundable once consumed. Unused credits at the time of a refund are forfeited. Free starter credits (500) are non-refundable as they have no monetary value.

Need help first?

Many issues can be resolved by adjusting element selectors, rules, or browser permissions. Before requesting a refund, we encourage you to reach out — we're happy to help.
