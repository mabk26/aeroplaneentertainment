Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 12, 2026 · Aeroplane Entertainment

This Privacy Policy explains how Aeroplane Entertainment ("we", "us", or "our") handles data for the CLICKO Chrome extension and associated website. CLICKO is designed so your automation data stays local in your browser.

Information we collect

  • Extension data: Picked elements, rules, URL profiles, variables, settings, training files, and logs are stored locally using Chrome's chrome.storage.local API on your own device. This data is not transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly use the Cloud Sync feature.
  • Account email: When you sign in with Google, your Google email address is used as your account identifier to verify your license and manage AI credits.
  • AI usage: When you use the Clicko Engine or AI Recommendations (CLICKO Cloud), the page snapshot and your task prompt are sent to our server and then to Google's Gemini API to generate a response. We do not permanently store these prompts or page snapshots.
  • Credit balance: Your AI credit balance and subscription status are stored on our server (Redis database) associated with your Google email.
  • Payment data: Payments are processed entirely by Lemon Squeezy. We receive webhook events containing your email, subscription status, and transaction identifiers needed for license activation and refund handling. We do not store credit card numbers or payment method details.

What we do not collect

  • We do not collect page contents, passwords, form data, or browsing history.
  • We do not sell your data to third parties.
  • We do not use your data for advertising.
  • Your automation configuration (elements, rules, profiles) stays on your device unless you use Cloud Sync.

Cloud Sync (optional)

If you choose to use Cloud Sync, your automation profile (elements and rules) is uploaded to our server under your verified Google email. You can delete this data at any time by contacting support.

Third-party services

  • Google OAuth: Used for sign-in. We receive your email and display name. Google's privacy policy applies: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Lemon Squeezy: Payment processor. Their privacy policy applies: lemonsqueezy.com/privacy
  • Google Gemini API: Powers AI features. Prompts are sent server-side using our API key. Google's API terms apply.

Data retention

License and credit data is retained while your account is active. If you cancel your subscription and request deletion, we will remove your server-side data within 30 days. Local extension data can be cleared at any time from Chrome's extension storage.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing us. We will respond within 30 days.
