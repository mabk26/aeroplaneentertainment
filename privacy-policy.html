Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Aeroplane Entertainment ("we" or "us") handles data for the CLICKO extension. CLICKO is designed so your automation data stays local in your browser.

Information we collect

  • Extension data: picked elements, rules, profiles, settings, variables, and logs are stored using Chrome local storage.
  • Account email: when you sign in with Google, your email is used to verify your license.
  • Payment data: payments are processed by Paddle. We receive license-related webhook events and store the email, status, and transaction identifiers needed for activation and refund handling.

What we do not collect

We do not collect page contents, passwords, form data, browsing history, or your automation configuration on our server.

How data is used

Your email is used only for login, license verification, customer support, and fraud/refund handling.
