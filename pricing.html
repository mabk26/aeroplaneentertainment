FAQ

Everything you need to know about CLICKO plans.

How do credits work?

1 credit ≈ $0.001 of AI usage. Each Engine step or AI scan costs credits based on the actual tokens used × 1.25 (a 25% margin). A typical Engine step costs about 10–30 credits depending on page size.

Do unused credits carry over?

Yes — credits never expire. A new month simply refills your pool on top of whatever is left. There is no reset.

How does activation work?

Sign in to the extension with Google, subscribe here with the same email, then click "Refresh License" in the extension profile menu. Your Pro features and credits activate instantly.

Can I use it on multiple devices?

Yes. Sign in with the same Google account on any device and click "Refresh License." Your license and credits follow your Google account.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel anytime from the Lemon Squeezy customer portal. You keep Pro access until the end of your billing period. We also offer a 14-day money-back guarantee — see our Refund Policy.

Do I need my own AI API key?

No. CLICKO Cloud handles all AI calls using credits — no API key needed. For the Recommendations scanner you can optionally bring your own Gemini key for free, unlimited use on your own quota.
