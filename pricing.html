FAQ

How do credits work?

1 credit ≈ $0.001 of AI usage. Each Engine step or AI scan costs credits based on the actual tokens used × 1.25 (a 25% margin). A typical Engine step costs about 10 credits.

Do unused credits carry over?

Yes — credits never expire. A new month simply refills your pool on top of whatever is left.

How does activation work?

Sign in to the extension with Google, subscribe here with the same email, then click "Refresh License" in the extension profile menu.

Can I use it on multiple devices?

Yes. Sign in with the same Google account and refresh the license.
